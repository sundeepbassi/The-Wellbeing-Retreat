What is Yoga?

Yoga is the ability to use ones body and mind to create wellness. The mental exercises will help to calm the mind and the physical exercises will help to Strengthen the body. Yoga Has been practiced for over 5000 years and has become a very popoular for self-improvement.

Yoga Techniques

  • Ashtanga
  • Vinyasa
  • ViniYoga
  • Child's Pose
  • Plank Pose
  • Tree Pose
  • Bridge Pose

A Yoga lesson

in this lesson we will learn to do a seated forward bend.

  • Sit comfortably with legs together
  • Feet must not be turned in or out.
  • Keep your hands by hips
  • Lift chest and move forward from the waist
  • Take 8 to 10 breathes
  • Now relax

What is Meditation?

Meditation uses a technique called mindfullness to help focus the mind on a particular object such as one's breathing. Meditation originated in Asia and is an ancient practice used within Buddhism and Hinduism

Meditation Techniques

  • Mindfullness
  • Focused
  • Progressive Relaxation
  • Movement
  • Transcental
  • Loving Kindness
  • Visualisation

A Meditation Lesson

In this lesson we will learn to do mindfullness meditation.

  • First sit comfortably
  • Relax and be in the present moment
  • Watch your breathe coming in and out
  • If thoughts arise just watch them
  • Let go of judgements
  • Do this for 10 mins
